实际执行的 SQL 语句（带参数）
SELECT sb.ts_code, sb.name, sid.close, sid.trade_date, (sid.close - COALESCE(prev.close, sid.close)) * 100.0 / NULLIF(COALESCE(prev.close, sid.close), 0) AS daily_change_pct, (sid.close * sid.volume) / 10000.0 AS turnover_10k FROM stock_indicator_daily sid LEFT JOIN stock_indicator_daily prev ON prev.stock_code = sid.stock_code AND prev.trade_date = (SELECT MAX(trade_date) FROM stock_indicator_daily WHERE stock_code = sid.stock_code AND trade_date < sid.trade_date) JOIN stock_basic sb ON sb.ts_code = sid.stock_code WHERE sid.trade_date = '20240115' AND (sid.ma_5 > sid.ma_20 AND prev.ma_5 <= prev.ma_20) -- MA5 cross_up MA20 AND (sid.volume > (sid.vol_ma_20 * 1.5)) -- VOLUME > VOL_MA20 * 1.5 AND sb.name NOT LIKE '%ST%' -- 剔除ST AND (sb.list_date IS NULL OR sb.list_date <= '20230115') -- 上市满1年 AND (sid.close * sid.volume) >= 50000000 -- 成交额>=5000万 ORDER BY (sid.close * sid.volume) DESC LIMIT 200;
查询性能：预计扫描 ~3000 行（单日），执行时间 < 200ms。如需更快，可在 stock_indicator_daily 表对 trade_date 字段建索引。
